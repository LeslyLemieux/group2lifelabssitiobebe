Chosen Youtube Category



→ Video Podcasts

→ Why? Well, it keeps people company. And even though it says “video” podcast,
the main focus here is just us talking about our topic. So really, people can listen
to it no matter what they’re doing. Bored? Listen to our podcast. Busy yet needs
something to play in the background? Listen to our podcast. Waiting in a long, boring
queue? Listen to our podcast. Not only can it be listened to by a wide variety of people
(may it be young people to adults) in various occasions, it’s also a light hearted way
to share the situation regarding Sitio Bebe. Video podcasts are also informative as they
discuss topics. This way, we can present more about their issue and the fundraising for it.

Target Audience



→ We have a couple audiences our content is targetted to:

  • Podcast enjoyers
  • High schoolers
  • Young adults (aged between 18 to 35)

    • → Those people are our target audiences because we think that with our content,
